Autonomous Drivering Research Paper

A paper about if autonomous driving will reshape the future.
Keywords—perception, uncertainty, connected stream, ethic.
Abstract—This paper aims to analyze some of the available and potential techniques to develop a safe autonomous driving experience. A combination of a variety of sensors are used to enable machine perception and learning algorithms are available to conquer the object classification problem. Uncertainties arise from perception are solvable by learning algorithms as well. In addition to operating as an individual driverless car, the connected steam of autonomous car has been developed to improve the safety on the road. Nevertheless, to accomplish the communication between the vehicles, some new security flaws are caught. On the aspect of ethic, the decisions made by an autonomous car could be controversial. Because of the imperfect of AI learning process, making the most ethical or least harmful decisions under any urgent situations could be hard for programmers. The further development on autonomous car could bring disruption on the economy due to the replacement of driver’s occupation and the reduced need for accident insurance.
